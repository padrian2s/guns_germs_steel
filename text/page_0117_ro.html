Rezumat

Accidental crop cultivation mechanisms. Latrines: concentration of wild plant seeds from human consumption. Humans unconsciously selecting seed sources through harvesting, transport, and disposal. Garbage and latrines creating cultivation microenvironments. Initial crop development entirely unintentional: humans simply concentrating viable seeds near settlements.

Concepte Cheie

Unconscious cultivation through human behavior. Seed spillage during collection and transport. Disposal of fruit containing viable seeds. Concentration of genetic material near human settlements. Natural seed germination in advantageous microhabitats. Humans unaware of selecting for cultivation-favorable traits.
