Rezumat

Failed domestication examples demonstrating genetic barriers. Acorns: high tannin content required processing (grinding/leaching or mutation selection). Never domesticated despite prized food source. Strawberries/raspberries: long domestication delay until 20th century. What barriers prevented domestication of otherwise desirable wild foods?

Concepte Cheie

Domestication barriers beyond simple cultivation. Toxin content preventing easy domestication. Low-toxin mutation rarity limiting domestication speed. Beast predation reducing harvest efficiency (bird/animal predation on ripening berries). Genetic variation insufficiency for overcoming barriers. Some wild plants unsuitable domestication candidates despite desirability.
