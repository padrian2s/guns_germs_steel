Key Concepts

Unconscious selection: human practice (harvesting, replanting largest seeds) automatically selects domestication traits without intentional breeding program. Wild plant traits: small seeds, hard seed coat, seed dispersal mechanism, germination variation. Domestication traits: larger seeds, soft seed coat, synchronized germination, loss seed dispersal (seeds don't scatter, making harvest possible).
