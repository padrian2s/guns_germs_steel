Rezumat

African animals occasionally tamed (Hannibal elephants, Egyptian giraffes) but never domesticated. Domestication requires selective breeding in captivity, genetic modification for human utility. Africa lacked domesticated megafauna unlike Eurasia (horses, cattle, camels). Animals unsuitable for domestication prevented development of animal-based technologies (cavalry, carts, plowing).
