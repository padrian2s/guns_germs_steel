Concepte Cheie

Crop domestication timing reflects genetic constraints. Apples genetics require grafting: not spontaneously domesticable through simple selection. Grain crops: spontaneously domesticate through seed germination selection. Genetic traits determine domestication feasibility timeline independent of human capability.
