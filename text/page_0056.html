Summary

Chathams: small, remote, supporting only ~2,000 hunter-gatherers. Moriori had no other islands to colonize. Instead developed conflict-prevention mechanisms: renounced warfare, reduced overpopulation through male infanticide. Contrasts sharply with New Zealand North's warmth and size supporting large farmer population with chronic warfare.
