Rezumat

Based on 33 years with New Guineans, Diamond argues they are genetically more intelligent than Westerners. He explains two mechanisms: (1) Natural selection in Europe favored disease resistance genes (not intelligence) because epidemic diseases killed regardless of intelligence; in New Guinea, murder and warfare killed the unintelligent, selecting for intelligence. (2) New Guinean children experience intensive active stimulation versus Western children's passive TV consumption, boosting mental development.
