Rezumat

Chapter 10 examines continental axis orientations' impact on technology diffusion. Americas: north-south axis (9,000 miles N-S vs. 3,000 miles E-W maximum, 40 miles at Panama). Africa: also primarily north-south but less extreme. Eurasian continents: primarily east-west orientation. Geographic axis orientation determines technology diffusion speed.

Concepte Cheie

Continental axis orientation determines technology diffusion feasibility. East-west axes facilitate technology movement: similar climates/day-length/seasons. North-south axes hinder technology movement: crossing diverse climates/growing seasons. Geographic orientation physical constraint on agricultural technology diffusion.
