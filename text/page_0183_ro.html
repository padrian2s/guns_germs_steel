Rezumat

Crops never domesticated independently after Fertile Crescent origin. Genetic evidence: no multiple domestication mutations indicating independent domestication elsewhere. If independently domesticated multiple times, would show varied chromosomal arrangements/mutations. Single genetic origin proves diffusion, not independent invention. Concepte Cheie

Genetic signatures reveal domestication origin. Multiple mutations = independent domestications. Single mutations = single origin diffusion. Preemptive domestication: rapid diffusion prevents independent domestication elsewhere. Economic logic: why domesticate inferior crop if superior crop available via diffusion? Genetic evidence proves diffusion dominance over independent invention.
