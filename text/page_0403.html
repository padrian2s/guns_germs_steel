Key Concepts

Historical science: explaining observed patterns through mechanistic understanding. Geographic principles enable prediction: given geographic data could predict civilization development. History as testable science: geographic determinism generates falsifiable predictions. Methodology: systematically comparing geographic factors across societies testing explanatory power.

Epilogue addresses whether such predictions hold explanatory power across cases. Geographic framework enables systematic historical testing.
