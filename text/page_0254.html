Summary

Innovation receptivity is not a continental characteristic. Every continent contains both innovative and conservative societies coexisting. Innovation receptivity fluctuates temporally within same regions. Societies become more innovative or conservative over time based on changing conditions. This pattern across all continents proves innovation depends on multiple independent factors (economic, geographic, demographic), not continental ideology or cultural traits.
