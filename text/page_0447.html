Key Concepts

Band organization: small kin groups, egalitarian, no permanent leadership. Tribe organization: agricultural village communities, egalitarian/ranked, consensual leadership. Chiefdom organization: hierarchical regional organization, chief leadership, tribute/labor extraction. State organization: complex hierarchical bureaucracy, institutionalized government, law codes, military.
