Rezumat

Domestication transformation from dual mechanisms: Conscious farmer selection (choosing seeds for replanting) and unconscious natural selection (favorable traits survive cultivation). Darwin's natural selection concept applies within cultivation: certain plant individuals survive/reproduce better under cultivation. Both selection types converge toward domestication traits.

Concepte Cheie

Dual selection mechanisms. Conscious harvest decisions creating artificial selection. Unconscious natural selection through cultivation environment. Traits advantageous in wild (dispersal, dormancy) disadvantageous in cultivation. Traits disadvantageous in wild (large seeds, dormancy reduction) advantageous in cultivation. Selection pressure convergence accelerates domestication.
