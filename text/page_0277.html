Summary

Kleptocratic solutions maintaining elite power: 1) Disarm populace, arm elite (modern weapons monopoly easier than ancient spears). 2) Redistribute tribute to masses in popular ways to maintain happiness. Government complexity enables solutions (technology differentiation, taxation systems, redistribution administration). Geographic population density and technological development determine kleptocratic feasibility.

Historical & Geographic Context

Industrial states (modern Europe, US): monopolized high-tech weapons production. Ancient states: monopolized rare metals (bronze, iron) through control of mines and craftspeople. Agricultural empires: maintained control through taxation administration and redistribution. Hunter-gatherers: impossible kleptocratic extraction due to lack of storable surplus and homogeneous weapon availability.

Connection to Main Thesis
