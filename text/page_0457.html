Historical & Geographic Context

Mayr distinguished historical science (evolution, geology, astronomy, history) from nonhistorical science (physics) regarding cause-effect determination. Evolution biology example: comparing species, biogeographies, fossil records to identify causal patterns. Human history methodology analogous: comparing continents, societies, geographies to identify causal geographic factors. Book's continental comparisons follow Mayr framework: historical science methodology identifying geographic determinism through comparative analysis rather than experimentation.
